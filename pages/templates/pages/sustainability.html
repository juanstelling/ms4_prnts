Sustainability

CREATING A MORE BEAUTIFUL WORLD

Sustainability is close to our heart. Our products wouldn’t exists without the natureal materials from the forest. Creating beauty through art is our reason for being, and to do so sustainably means constantly striving to improve our production process and actively reducing its impact on our envoronment. For an even more beautiful world.

Caring for both environment and society

As of 2020 we are a Forest Stewardship Council® (FSC) certified company, a natural step in our journey towards our sustainability goals. FSC is a non-profit organisation that sets certain high standards to ensure forestry is practiced in an environmentally responsible, socially beneficial way.

Art printed on environmentally certified paper

We are proud to work with an eco-conscious paper mill. Our paper mill is FSC certified. All of our posters are certified and labelled with the FSC Mix symbol on our site, which guarantees that at least 70% of the wood used in their production is from FSC certified forests.
